# 🎬 LIFELINE AI — Live 3-Minute Demo Playbook

This playbook outlines the exact step-by-step path to demonstrate **LIFELINE AI**'s premium value proposition during a 3-minute hackathon pitch or review.

---

## ⏱️ Pre-Flight Setup
1. Launch the application in a fresh browser window.
2. Ensure you have activated **Demo Mode** using the floating toggle in the navbar or accessing:
   `http://localhost:3000/?demo=true`
3. Hover over the circular **Lifeline Score™** wheels in the backlog to inspect calculations.

---

## 🏃‍♂️ Step-by-Step Walkthrough

### **Minute 1: The Problem & Dynamic Prioritization**
* **Say**: "As professionals, we are drowning in messy backlogs where priority is guessed, not calculated. Deadlines slip because urgency is invisible."
* **Action**:
  1. Point out the pre-seeded backlog tasks with varying **Lifeline Scores** (e.g. 88, 72, 45).
  2. Complete the overdue task: **"Setup Google Cloud Run production environment"**.
  3. Notice the **streak counter** incrementing in the navbar with a beautiful flame icon, and the colorful **canvas-confetti** explosion!

### **Minute 2: Real-time AI Urgency Intelligence & Brain-Dump**
* **Say**: "Lifeline AI uses a combination of mathematical algorithms and context-aware Gemini Flash agents to analyze tasks and extract micro next steps."
* **Action**:
  1. Click the **Brain icon** on the task **"Review production database security rules"**.
  2. Notice the sleek **AI Analysis Drawer** sliding open. Show the judge:
     * **Dynamic AI Urgency rating** (e.g., 72).
     * **Advanced AI Strategic reasoning**.
     * **Blockers and dependencies list**.
     * **Low-Friction Next Action Step**: *"Isolate the first module... spent 15 minutes drafting..."*
  3. Click **"Capture Task"** on top and select **"AI Bulk Brain-Dump"**.
  4. Paste this raw text: *"meeting with Arpit on Wednesday about the slider layout, also buy dark espresso coffee beans tonight"*
  5. Click **"Deconstruct Brain-Dump"**. Watch Gemini parse and extract structured tasks in real-time.
  6. Click **"Import"** to pull them directly into your dynamic backlog!

### **Minute 3: Tactical Focus & Weekly Strategizer**
* **Say**: "When cognitive fatigue strikes, Lifeline AI's Focus Mode reduces the noise. It isolates the single highest priority task and enlists an AI Coach to help you start."
* **Action**:
  1. Switch to the **Tactical Focus** tab from the sidebar.
  2. Notice that the highest-score task is isolated, complete with a clean **Pomodoro Timer**.
  3. Click **"Activate AI Focus Coach"**.
  4. Show the judge the atomic breakdown checklist and the high-energy motivational statement tailored to banish procrastination!
  5. Toggle to the **"Weekly Strategy"** sub-tab.
  6. Click **"Synthesize Strategy"**. Watch Gemini 1.5 Pro compile your open tasks into an hour-by-hour Markdown calendar.
  7. Click **"Export Strategic PDF"** to demonstrate the fully branded PDF export.
